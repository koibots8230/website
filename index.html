    About Us

    Team 8230, the Koibots, is a high school FIRST Robotics Competition team from Arlington, Virginia. Founded in 2019, we have competed at multiple FIRST events and collaborated with other teams across the state. Members of team 8230 are committed to learning about the world of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) through hands-on experiences and opportunities, which includes building robots. As Koibots, we strive to learn, lead, and represent our school, Arlington Tech, as young scientists and engineers.
